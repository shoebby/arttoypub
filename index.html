Art Toy

/ɑːt tɔɪ/

In my Thesis Cannons, Dynamite Erasers, and Screaming Into The Void I define 'Art Toys' as digital art-making tools that feature playful and exploratory modes of interaction that prioritise the process of making rather than the creation of a final product, and for the purposes of this mini-mag that's still generally correct!

An additional note to make is how these tool-toys often aim to reimagine the very act of making something digitally.

And this is another one!
